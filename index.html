What We Do

No job too complex.
No shortcut taken.

Konrad brings master-level precision to every job — the kind of work dealerships and chain shops won't touch, done right the first time.

Engine Rebuilds

Full teardown, precision machining, and hand assembly. M104, M119, OM606, and beyond — brought back to spec or better.

Transmission Rebuilds

Complete 722.6 NAG1 and automatic gearbox rebuilds. Proper friction kits, solenoids, and valve body work — not a band-aid.

Timing Chain Service

Chain, guides, tensioners, and VVT components replaced to factory spec. Prevent catastrophic failure before it starts.

Electrical Diagnosis

Factory wiring diagrams, module programming, harness repair. We trace faults to the source — not the symptom.

OBD & Diagnostic Scanning

Autel and Mercedes-specific diagnostic tools. Full system scans, live data analysis, and fault code interpretation — not a guessing game.

Brake Service

Rotors, pads, calipers, and brake line work. Quality parts only — because stopping power isn't where you cut corners.

Suspension & Steering

Knuckles, control arms, tie rods, bushings, and alignment prep. Restore the precise feel these cars were engineered to deliver.

Cooling System

Radiators, water pumps, thermostats, expansion tanks, and hose replacement. A healthy cooling system protects everything downstream.

Pre-Purchase Inspections

Know exactly what you're buying before money changes hands. We document every fault, every deferred job, every red flag.
